# Article Draft Prompt v1

Used by `lib/jobs/process-articles.ts` (inline — this file is reference documentation).

## Filter Logic

The LLM must first decide whether an article is substantive enough to process.

**Skip criteria** (return `{"skip":true,"skipReason":"..."}` if any apply):
- Pure company earnings / financial reporting with no AI product news
- Sports, entertainment, or lifestyle content unrelated to AI
- Duplicate of a story already processed with no new information
- Marketing fluff with no concrete product or research announcement

**Required fields when skip=false**:
1. `skip: false`
2. Content fields (titleZh / summaryZh, tag, aiInsights)
3. `aiInsights.oneSentenceSummary` — must be a concrete statement, not a generic intro

## Anti-Cliché Rules

Do not use:
- R1: "引领", "赋能", "革命性", "颠覆"
- R2: "随着 AI 的发展", "AI 时代", "未来已来"
- W1: Vague phrases like "影响深远", "意义重大" without specifics
- W2: Opening with "本文", "这篇文章"
